FAQ

Do I need a developer?

No. Deploy to GitHub Pages, click the ⚙️ admin button, and fill in your org name/email/colors.

Can I preview before deploying?

Yes—use the "View Template" button on any template card.

How do I integrate with internal systems?

Start with exports (CSV/JSON). For larger orgs, replace Fireproof with your store and POST to your ingestion endpoints—each template's README lists touch points.
