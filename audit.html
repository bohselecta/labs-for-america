Audit Guide

Coming soon. This guide explains the audit and transparency features of LabsForAmerica templates.

Audit Features

  • Timestamped logs: All actions are recorded with precise timestamps
  • Printable summaries: Generate clean reports for meetings and records
  • Structured exports: CSV/JSON exports for integration with internal systems
  • Participation tracking: Monitor engagement and turnout metrics
  • Decision trails: Track how decisions were made and by whom

Compliance & Transparency

  • All data exports include metadata and timestamps
  • Audit logs are tamper-evident
  • Printable reports maintain formatting for official records
  • Integration points clearly documented in each template's README
